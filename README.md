🌸 GlowNest Naturals

Nurture Your Glow, Naturally.

GlowNest Naturals is a premium skincare brand dedicated to helping women restore confidence through gentle, nature-inspired skincare rituals. This project showcases a modern conversion-focused landing page designed to attract, educate, and convert skincare-conscious women seeking healthier-looking, radiant skin.

✨ Project Overview

The website was created to support GlowNest Naturals' marketing funnel by:

- Capturing leads through a free skincare guide
- Building trust with potential customers
- Educating visitors about skincare habits
- Promoting the Glow Recovery Kit
- Increasing conversions and customer engagement

🎯 Target Audience

- Women aged 22–40
- Melanin-rich skin types
- Individuals dealing with dark spots, acne scars, and uneven skin tone
- Customers seeking gentle, natural skincare alternatives
